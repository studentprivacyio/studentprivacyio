# Privacy Policy

At Student Privacy, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines our approach to data collection and usage.

## Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our visitors. Our website is designed to be as privacy-respecting as possible.

## Analytics

We use Cloudflare's server-side analytics to understand general website traffic and performance metrics. This analytics service:
- Does not use cookies
- Does not track individual users
- Does not collect personal information
- Only provides aggregate statistics about website visits

The analytics data helps us ensure the website runs smoothly and efficiently without compromising your privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
